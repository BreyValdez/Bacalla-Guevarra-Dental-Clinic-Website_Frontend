What is Restoration?

Dental restoration is the process of repairing or replacing damaged or missing teeth. It involves a variety of procedures, including fillings to repair tooth decay, crowns to cap damaged teeth, bridges to replace missing teeth, and dental implants to support artificial teeth. These procedures not only restore the function of your teeth but also improve your overall oral health and appearance.

You may need dental restoration if you have:
     ⦁ Tooth decay
     ⦁ Chipped or cracked teeth
     ⦁ Missing teeth
     ⦁ Worn-down teeth

Types of Dental Restoration:
     ⦁ Fillings
     ⦁ Crowns
     ⦁ Bridges
     ⦁ Dental Implants
