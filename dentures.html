What is Tooth Dentures?

Dentures are removable dental appliances that replace missing teeth and restore your smile. They are made of acrylic or metal and are designed to fit snugly over your gums. Dentures can be either partial or full.

⦁ Partial dentures replace some missing teeth and are held in place by clasps that attach to your existing teeth.
⦁ Full dentures replace all of the teeth in your upper or lower jaw.

Dentures can improve your appearance, speech, and chewing ability. They can also help prevent facial muscle sagging and maintain the shape of your face.
