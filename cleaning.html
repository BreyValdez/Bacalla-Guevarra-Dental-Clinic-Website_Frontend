What is cleaning and when you should undergo this?

Cleaning in the context of dental care refers to the process of removing plaque and tartar from your teeth and gums. Plaque is a sticky film that forms on your teeth and contains bacteria. Tartar is hardened plaque that can build up along your gumline.

You should undergo regular dental cleanings for the following reasons:

     ⦁ To prevent gum disease
     ⦁ To prevent cavities
     ⦁ To detect early signs of oral health problems
     ⦁ To maintain a bright smile

Adults have professional cleanings every six months. However, the frequency of your cleanings may vary depending on your individual oral health needs.
