What is Tooth Extraction?

Tooth extraction is a dental procedure involving the removal of a tooth from the gum socket, often performed under local or general anesthesia. Following post-extraction care instructions is crucial for proper healing and preventing complications.

You might need a tooth extraction in the following situations:

     ⦁ Severe tooth decay
     ⦁ Impacted wisdom teeth
     ⦁ Injury or traumabr>
     ⦁ Periodontal disease
     ⦁ Crowding

If you experience any dental pain, swelling, or other concerning symptoms, it's important to consult first with our dentist to determine the best course of treatment.
