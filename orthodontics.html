What is Orthodontics?

Orthodontics is a specialized field of dentistry that focuses on correcting misaligned teeth and jaws. It involves using braces, aligners, or other orthodontic appliances to improve the appearance and function of your teeth and bite. Common orthodontic issues include crooked teeth, overcrowding, underbite, overbite, and gaps between teeth.
